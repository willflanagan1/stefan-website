Pulse Oximeter

Completed Assembly

The project was apart of my BME 310 design class. I was put into a group of four. We had to take apart a pulse oximeter and reverse engineer the device. We broke the device into its respective parts (shown to the right). We designed the parts of the pulse oximeter on Solidworks and put them into an assembly.
